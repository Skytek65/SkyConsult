Our Story

Family-Owned Greek-American Tradition

Grecian Delight is a small, family-owned Greek-American style restaurant serving up delicious, traditional Greek dishes made with fresh, high-quality ingredients. From savory gyros and flavorful moussaka to crisp Greek salads and decadent baklava, we offer a taste of Greece in the heart of Middletown.

Our cozy, inviting atmosphere and friendly service create the perfect setting to enjoy an authentic Greek dining experience. Every dish is prepared with love and recipes passed down through generations.
