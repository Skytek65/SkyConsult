Our Story

A Cookie Tradition Since 1983

Blue Chip Cookies was founded on a simple belief: the best cookies come from patience, quality ingredients, and respect for tradition. We bake in small batches using time-honored recipes—no frozen dough, no shortcuts, no fleeting trends.

Our cookies are perfectly sized, thoughtfully flavored, and crafted to deliver balance in every bite. We use Guittard chocolate, AA butter, and pure vanilla—the same ingredients, the same care, 43 years running.

Whether you're sending two cookies or a fully customized 14-cookie assortment, every Blue Chip gift feels intentional, polished, and memorable.
