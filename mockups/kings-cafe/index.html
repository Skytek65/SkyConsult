Our Story

Breakfast Royalty in Mason

At Kings Cafe, we believe breakfast is the most important meal of the day - and it should be treated like royalty. Every morning, we open our doors at 7 AM to serve the finest breakfast and brunch to the wonderful people of Mason, Ohio.

Our menu features classic breakfast favorites with a royal twist. From our famous French Toast Puffs to our hearty Royal King Platter, every dish is made with fresh ingredients and served with a smile. Whether you're starting your workday or enjoying a lazy weekend brunch, we've got the perfect meal waiting for you.
