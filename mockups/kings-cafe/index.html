Our Story

Brewing Community,
One Cup at a Time

Kings Cafe has been a beloved gathering place in Mason, Ohio since 2009. What started as a small coffee cart has grown into a cozy neighborhood cafe where friends meet, students study, and families create memories.

We source our beans from ethical, sustainable farms and roast them to perfection. Our pastries are baked fresh every morning, and our lunch menu features locally-sourced ingredients whenever possible. At Kings Cafe, you're not just a customer — you're family.
