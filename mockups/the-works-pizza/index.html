Step into The Works Pizza Co., a beloved family-owned restaurant nestled in a historically rich building dating back to 1905. Once a water filling station and firehouse, this iconic establishment has been serving Downtown Loveland for over two decades.

Our General Manager, Tasha, has been with us for the entire 21 years, ensuring every pizza that comes out of our iconic rotating brick oven meets our exacting standards. The Works radiates with restored charm and character, creating a warm and inviting atmosphere where families and friends gather to enjoy handcrafted culinary masterpieces.
