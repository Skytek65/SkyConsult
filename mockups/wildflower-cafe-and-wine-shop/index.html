Our Story

A labor of love nestled in a charming 1800s farmhouse, where every dish tells a story of local farmers and passionate craftsmanship.

Farm-Fresh, Heartfelt Dining

Wildflower Café began with a simple vision: to create a gathering place where the community could experience the true taste of locally-sourced, seasonal ingredients. Set in a beautifully restored 1800s farmhouse, our restaurant honors the rich agricultural heritage of Mason, Ohio.

We partner with local farmers and artisans who share our commitment to quality and sustainability. From our morning pastries to our evening entrées, every dish reflects the best of what the season has to offer.
