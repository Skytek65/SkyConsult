Our Story

Authentic Mexican Tradition Since 2015

El Trompo Mexican Grill brings the vibrant flavors and warm hospitality of Mexico to Mason, Ohio. Our name honors the traditional vertical rotisserie (trompo) used to make the famous Tacos Al Pastor, a beloved street food from Mexico City.

Every dish we serve is prepared with authentic recipes passed down through generations. We use only the freshest ingredients, traditional spices, and time-honored cooking techniques to bring you a true taste of Mexico.
