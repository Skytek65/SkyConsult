Our Story

A Century of Flavor & History

Nestled in the heart of downtown Loveland, The Works occupies a historic building dating back to 1905. Once the power plant for the Loveland Electric Light and Power Company, this remarkable space has been transformed into one of the area's most unique dining destinations.

For over two decades, we've been serving handcrafted brick oven pizzas, authentic Italian dishes, and American classics in a truly one-of-a-kind atmosphere. Our iconic rotating brick oven, visible from the dining room, turns out perfectly blistered pizzas while you watch.

The Experience

More Than Just Dining

Step into a piece of Loveland history. Our unique atmosphere combines industrial charm with warm hospitality for an unforgettable experience.

Train Car Dining

Dine in our iconic converted train car! A truly unique experience that transports you while you enjoy your meal. Perfect for memorable dinners and special occasions.

Rotating Brick Oven

Watch our master pizzaiolos craft your pizza before your eyes. Our rotating brick oven ensures perfectly blistered crusts and authentically charred flavors every time.

Historic Firehouse

Our building dates to 1905 and retains its original industrial character. Big, open, and airy with plenty of room for large parties and intimate dinners alike.
