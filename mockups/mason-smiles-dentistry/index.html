Pediatric Dentistry

Gentle care for your little ones. We make dental visits fun and stress-free for children, creating positive associations that last a lifetime.

  • First dental visits (age 1+)
  • Sealants & fluoride
  • Orthodontic screening

Cosmetic Dentistry

Transform your smile with our advanced cosmetic treatments. From subtle enhancements to complete smile makeovers.

  • Professional teeth whitening
  • Porcelain veneers
  • Invisalign clear aligners

Restorative Dentistry

Repair and restore damaged or missing teeth with our modern restorative solutions that look and feel natural.

  • Tooth-colored fillings
  • Dental crowns & bridges
  • Dental implants

Preventive Care

Stay ahead of dental problems with regular checkups and professional cleanings tailored to your individual needs.

  • Routine cleanings & exams
  • Digital X-rays
  • Oral cancer screenings

Emergency Dentistry

Dental emergencies don't wait. We offer same-day appointments for urgent dental issues when you need us most.

  • Same-day appointments
  • Toothache relief
  • Broken tooth repair

Senior Dental Care

Specialized care for seniors including denture services, gum disease treatment, and age-related oral health management.

  • Dentures & partials
  • Gum disease treatment
  • Dry mouth solutions

About Us

Your Trusted Local Dental Practice

At Mason Smiles Family Dentistry, we believe everyone deserves a healthy, beautiful smile. Since opening our doors over 15 years ago, we've been dedicated to providing exceptional dental care with a gentle, patient-centered approach.

Our state-of-the-art facility combines the latest dental technology with a warm, welcoming environment. We understand that visiting the dentist can be stressful, which is why we've created a space where patients of all ages feel comfortable and cared for.
