Services We Offer

At Crutchfield Dental Group, patient satisfaction is our number one priority. We offer personalized dental care services using the latest technology.

General Dentistry

Comprehensive care including cleanings, exams, fillings, and preventive treatments to maintain your oral health.

  • Routine Cleanings & Exams
  • Fillings & Restorations
  • Preventive Care

Sedation Dentistry

Relax completely during your procedure with our safe and effective sedation options. Perfect for anxious patients.

  • IV Sedation Available
  • Anxiety-Free Experience
  • Complex Procedures

Implant Dentistry

Permanent tooth replacement solutions that look, feel, and function like natural teeth. Restore your smile today.

  • Single & Multiple Implants
  • Implant-Supported Dentures
  • Full Mouth Restoration

Cosmetic Dentistry

Transform your smile with our cosmetic treatments. From whitening to veneers, achieve the smile of your dreams.

  • Professional Whitening
  • Porcelain Veneers
  • Smile Makeovers

Wisdom Teeth Removal

Safe and comfortable wisdom teeth extractions. We provide detailed aftercare instructions for optimal healing.

  • Surgical Extraction
  • Sedation Options
  • Complete Aftercare

Emergency Dental Care

Dental emergency? We're here to help. Prompt treatment for toothaches, broken teeth, and other urgent issues.

  • Same-Day Appointments
  • Toothache Relief
  • Broken Tooth Repair
