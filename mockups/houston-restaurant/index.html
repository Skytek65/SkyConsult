About Houston Restaurant

A Tradition of American Culinary Excellence

Since 2009, Houston Restaurant has been Mason's favorite destination for authentic American cuisine. Our passion for quality ingredients and time-honored recipes has earned us a reputation for excellence that keeps our guests coming back.

From perfectly grilled steaks and fresh seafood to our famous burgers and comforting breakfast classics, every dish is prepared with care by our talented chefs. We source local ingredients whenever possible and take pride in delivering a dining experience that feels like home.
