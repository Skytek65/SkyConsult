OUR STORY

A Taste of Mexico
in the Heart of Mason

El Trompo Mexican Grill was born from a passion for authentic Mexican cuisine and a dream to share our family's recipes with the Mason community. Named after the traditional vertical spit used to cook succulent meats, we bring the vibrant flavors and warm hospitality of Mexico to every plate we serve.

Every dish is crafted with care, using only the freshest ingredients and time-honored cooking techniques passed down through generations. From our hand-pressed tortillas to our house-made salsas, we take pride in serving food that tastes like home.
