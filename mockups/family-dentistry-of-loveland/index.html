Your Safety Is Our Priority

Office Sterilization & Safety

We want to ensure the health of our patients and our dental team. Our practice follows safety procedures recommended by OSHA, EPA, and CDC.

  • Disinfectant hand soap protocols
  • Gloves, masks, glasses & face shields
  • Chemical disinfection of all surfaces
  • Sterilization of all instruments
  • Disposable coverings for all equipment
