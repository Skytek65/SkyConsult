Miranda's Sun Spot

Kick back and relax on our spacious back patio, perfect for enjoying warm weather with an ice cream cone or a delicious meal. With ample seating, a pergola, fire pit, lawn games, and more, it's the ideal hangout for families, friends, and fun. Our patio is available for rent for birthdays, weddings, anniversaries, graduations, sports team meetings, baby showers, family reunions, or just kicking back with friends. We can accommodate up to 100 guests and we cater too!
