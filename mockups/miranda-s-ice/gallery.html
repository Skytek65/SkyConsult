Miranda's Patio at Your Disposal

Come enjoy warm weather and an ice cream cone or some food on our spacious back patio.

With ample seating, a beautiful pergola, fire pit, lawn games and so much more, our patio is the perfect place to unwind with family and friends.

  • Ample seating for large groups
  • Shaded pergola area

  • Lawn games and activities
  • Horse-friendly with tie-up space
  • E-bike charging stations nearby
