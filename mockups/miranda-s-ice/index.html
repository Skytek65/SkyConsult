Your Neighborhood Spot for Fun, Flavor, and Relaxation

Whether you're enjoying time with family and friends, treating yourself to a sweet escape, or taking a break after a bike ride, Miranda's is the perfect place to unwind.

We proudly serve premium gourmet ice cream in a variety of delicious flavors, along with a tasty selection of food, refreshments, sports drinks, and snacks. Choose from cups, classic cones, sugar cones, or waffle cones to enjoy your favorite scoop just the way you like it!

Miranda's Ice Cream Shoppe is owned and operated by sisters-in-law Olga and Anne. Olga discovered the previously-owned Miranda's Old Time Ice Cream while horseback riding through Morrow. She and Anne decided it was the perfect location to open a gathering place for family and friends to meet, laugh, and enjoy gourmet ice cream.

Situated on the Little Miami Scenic Trail, Miranda's serves families, bicyclists, canoeists, hikers, horseback riders and lovers of delicious gourmet ice cream.

The Miranda's Experience

🌿

Patio Paradise

Soak up the sun on our spacious back patio, perfect for enjoying warm weather with an ice cream cone or a delicious meal. With ample seating, a pergola, fire pit, lawn games, and more, it's the ideal hangout for families, friends, and fun.

🏦

Dine in a Vault

Yes—it's true! Miranda's is located in a charming historic bank building, and we've kept the original vault right in our dining room. Come experience a unique blend of history and hospitality.

☔

Rain or Shine

When the weather doesn't cooperate, our cozy Event Room is ready to welcome you. Whether it's too hot or raining outside, you can relax indoors in comfort and style.

🚴‍♀️

Trail Riders Welcome

Riding in on two wheels? We've got you covered with electric bike charging stations right outside! 🐴 Coming by horseback? We're proud to be a horse-friendly stop, with space to safely tie up and relax while you enjoy your treat.

🎉

Host Your Event

Our Patio and Event Room are available for private rentals. From birthdays and graduations to team parties, weddings, baby showers, family reunions, or casual get-togethers, we're here to make your event special.

Event Room: Seats up to 40 guests
Back Patio: Accommodates up to 100 guests

💕

Giving Back

In the spirit of making a difference and giving back to the community, Miranda's donates a portion of proceeds to organizations that help the victims of domestic violence.
