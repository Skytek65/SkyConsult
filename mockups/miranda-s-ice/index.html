Our Story

Owned and operated by sisters-in-law Olga and Anne, Miranda's Ice Cream Shoppe was born from a love of community and gourmet treats.

Olga discovered the previously-owned Miranda's Old Time Ice Cream while horseback riding through Morrow. She and Anne decided it was the perfect location to open a gathering place for family and friends to meet, laugh, and enjoy premium gourmet ice cream.

Situated on the Little Miami Scenic Trail, we proudly serve families, bicyclists, canoeists, hikers, horseback riders, and lovers of delicious gourmet ice cream.

— Olga and Anne
