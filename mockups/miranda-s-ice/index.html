Our Story

Miranda's Ice Cream Shoppe is owned and operated by sisters-in-law Olga and Anne.

Olga discovered the previously-owned Miranda's Old Time Ice Cream while horseback riding through Morrow. She and Anne decided it was the perfect location to open a gathering place for family and friends to meet, laugh, and enjoy gourmet ice cream.

Situated on the Little Miami Scenic Trail, Miranda's serves families, bicyclists, canoeists, hikers, horseback riders, and lovers of delicious gourmet ice cream.

— Olga and Anne

In the spirit of making a difference and giving back to the community, Miranda's donates a portion of proceeds to organizations that help the victims of domestic violence.

Experience the Charm

From our historic bank vault to our spacious patio, every corner of Miranda's offers something special.

🏦

Dine in a Vault

Yes—it's true! Miranda's is located in a charming historic bank building, and we've kept the original vault right in our dining room. Come experience a unique blend of history and hospitality.

🌿

Patio Paradise

Soak up the sun on our spacious back patio, perfect for enjoying warm weather with an ice cream cone or a delicious meal. Features ample seating, a pergola, fire pit, lawn games, and more.

🚴‍♀️

Trail Riders Welcome

Riding in on two wheels? We've got you covered with electric bike charging stations right outside! Coming by horseback? We're proud to be a horse-friendly stop with space to safely tie up and relax.
