Welcome to Miranda's

Miranda's Ice Cream Shoppe is owned and operated by sisters-in-law Olga and Anne. Olga discovered the previously-owned Miranda's Old Time Ice Cream while horseback riding through Morrow. She and Anne decided it was the perfect location to open a gathering place for family and friends to meet, laugh, and enjoy gourmet ice cream.

Situated on the Little Miami Scenic Trail, we serve families, bicyclists, canoeists, hikers, horseback riders and lovers of delicious gourmet ice cream.

— Olga and Anne

Patio Paradise

Soak up the sun on our spacious back patio with ample seating, a pergola, fire pit, lawn games, and more. Accommodates up to 100 guests for events.

🏦

Dine in a Vault

Located in a charming historic bank building, we've kept the original vault right in our dining room. Experience history and hospitality combined.

🚴‍♀️

Trail Friendly

Riding in on two wheels? We've got electric bike charging stations. Coming by horseback? We're a horse-friendly stop with space to safely tie up.

🎉

Host Your Event

Our Patio and Event Room are available for private rentals. Birthdays, weddings, graduations, reunions – we accommodate up to 100 guests.
