Our Story

Two Sisters-in-Law, One Sweet Dream

A Historic Bank Turned Sweet Retreat

Miranda's Ice Cream Shoppe is owned and operated by sisters-in-law Olga and Anne. Olga discovered the previously-owned Miranda's Old Time Ice Cream while horseback riding through Morrow. She and Anne decided it was the perfect location to open a gathering place for family and friends to meet, laugh, and enjoy gourmet ice cream.

Situated on the Little Miami Scenic Trail, we proudly serve families, bicyclists, canoeists, hikers, horseback riders and lovers of delicious gourmet ice cream. We're located in a charming historic bank building, and we've kept the original vault right in our dining room—come experience a unique blend of history and hospitality!

— Olga and Anne

Giving Back: Miranda's donates a portion of proceeds to organizations that help the victims of domestic violence.

What We Offer

More Than Just Ice Cream

🌿

Patio Paradise

Soak up the sun on our spacious back patio, perfect for enjoying warm weather with an ice cream cone or a delicious meal. Features ample seating, a pergola, fire pit, lawn games, and more. Accommodates up to 100 guests for private events.

🏦

Dine in a Vault

Yes—it's true! Our historic bank building features the original vault right in our dining room. Experience a unique blend of history and hospitality you won't find anywhere else.

☔

Rain or Shine

When the weather doesn't cooperate, our cozy Event Room seats up to 40 guests and is ready to welcome you. Whether it's too hot or raining outside, relax indoors in comfort and style.

🚴

Trail Riders Welcome

Riding in on two wheels? We've got you covered with electric bike charging stations right outside! Located directly on the Little Miami Scenic Trail.

🐴

Horse Friendly

Coming by horseback? No problem—we're proud to be a horse-friendly stop, with space to safely tie up and relax while you enjoy your treat.

🎉

Host Your Event

Our Patio and Event Room are both available for private rentals. From birthdays and graduations to team parties, weddings, baby showers, and family reunions.
