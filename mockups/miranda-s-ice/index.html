Your Go-To Spot for Sunshine, Good Vibes, and Great Food

Whether you're enjoying time with family and friends, treating yourself to a sweet escape, or taking a break after a bike ride, Miranda's is the perfect place to unwind.

We proudly serve premium gourmet ice cream in a variety of delicious flavors, along with a tasty selection of food, refreshments, sports drinks, and snacks. Choose from cups, classic cones, sugar cones, or waffle cones to enjoy your favorite scoop just the way you like it!

The Miranda's Experience

More than just ice cream — discover what makes our historic location special

🌿

Patio Paradise

Soak up the sun on our spacious back patio, perfect for enjoying warm weather with an ice cream cone or a delicious meal. Features ample seating, a pergola, fire pit, lawn games, and more. Accommodates up to 100 guests for private events.

☔

Rain or Shine

When the weather doesn't cooperate, our cozy Event Room is ready to welcome you. Whether it's too hot or raining outside, relax indoors in comfort and style. Seats up to 40 guests.

🏦

Dine in a Vault

Yes — it's true! Miranda's is located in a charming historic bank building, and we've kept the original vault right in our dining room. Come experience a unique blend of history and hospitality.

🎉

Host Your Event

Our Patio and Event Room are both available for private rentals. From birthdays and graduations to team parties, weddings, baby showers, and family reunions — we're here to make your event special.

About the Owners

Miranda's Ice Cream Shoppe is owned and operated by sisters-in-law Olga and Anne. Olga discovered the previously-owned Miranda's Old Time Ice Cream while horseback riding through Morrow. She and Anne decided it was the perfect location to open a gathering place for family and friends to meet, laugh, and enjoy gourmet ice cream.

In the spirit of making a difference, Miranda's donates a portion of proceeds to organizations that help the victims of domestic violence.
