How It All Began

Miranda's Ice Cream Shoppe is owned and operated by sisters-in-law Olga and Anne. The story began when Olga discovered the previously-owned Miranda's Old Time Ice Cream while horseback riding through the charming town of Morrow.

"She and Anne decided it was the perfect location to open a gathering place for family and friends to meet, laugh, and enjoy gourmet ice cream."

Situated right on the Little Miami Scenic Trail, Miranda's quickly became more than just an ice cream shop—it became a destination. Today, we proudly serve families, bicyclists, canoeists, hikers, horseback riders, and lovers of delicious gourmet ice cream from all over the region.

Our Unique Space

History meets hospitality in the heart of Morrow

🏦 The Historic Vault

Yes—it's true! Miranda's is located in a charming historic bank building, and we've kept the original vault right in our dining room. Come experience a unique blend of history and hospitality that you won't find anywhere else.

🌿 Patio Paradise

Soak up the sun on our spacious back patio, perfect for enjoying warm weather with an ice cream cone or a delicious meal. With ample seating, a pergola, fire pit, lawn games, and more, it's the ideal hangout for families and friends.

☔ Rain or Shine

When the weather doesn't cooperate, our cozy Event Room is ready to welcome you. Whether it's too hot or raining outside, you can relax indoors in comfort and style, making Miranda's perfect year-round.

Making a Difference

In the spirit of making a difference and giving back to the community, Miranda's donates a portion of proceeds to organizations that help the victims of domestic violence.
