Our Story

Two sisters-in-law, one dream, and a historic bank building full of sweet possibilities

How It All Began

Miranda's Ice Cream Shoppe is owned and operated by sisters-in-law Olga and Anne. The story began when Olga discovered the previously-owned Miranda's Old Time Ice Cream while horseback riding through the charming town of Morrow.

She and Anne decided it was the perfect location to open a gathering place for family and friends to meet, laugh, and enjoy gourmet ice cream. Situated on the Little Miami Scenic Trail, Miranda's has become a beloved stop for families, bicyclists, canoeists, hikers, horseback riders, and lovers of delicious gourmet ice cream.

— Olga and Anne

Our Mission & Values

More than just ice cream—we're building community, one scoop at a time

🍦

Quality First

We proudly serve premium gourmet ice cream in a variety of delicious flavors, crafted with care and served with love. From cups to classic cones, sugar cones to waffle cones, every scoop is perfection.

🤝

Community Giving

In the spirit of making a difference, Miranda's donates a portion of proceeds to organizations that help the victims of domestic violence. We believe in using our success to support those in need.

🏛️

Historic Charm

Located in a charming historic bank building, we've preserved the original vault right in our dining room. Experience a unique blend of history and hospitality you won't find anywhere else.

🚴

Trail Welcome

Whether you're on two wheels, four hooves, or two feet, you're welcome here. With bike charging stations and horse-friendly facilities, we're proud to serve the outdoor community.

A Space Like No Other

Step inside and you'll immediately feel the difference. Our historic bank building offers an atmosphere that transports you back in time while serving up modern gourmet treats.

🏦 The Vault

Yes—it's true! You can dine inside our original bank vault, making every visit a unique experience you won't find at any other ice cream shop.

🌿 Patio Paradise

Our spacious back patio features a pergola, fire pit, lawn games, and seating for up to 100 guests—perfect for events or relaxing with friends in the sunshine.

Rain or shine, our cozy Event Room seats up to 40 guests and is available for private rentals for birthdays, showers, reunions, and more.
