Our Story

Sweet Beginnings in Morrow

Two sisters-in-law, one dream, and a historic bank building turned gathering place for the community.

How It All Started

Miranda's Ice Cream Shoppe is owned and operated by sisters-in-law Olga and Anne. The story began when Olga discovered the previously-owned Miranda's Old Time Ice Cream while horseback riding through the charming town of Morrow.

She and Anne immediately recognized it as the perfect location to create something special—a gathering place where family and friends could meet, laugh, and enjoy premium gourmet ice cream together.

Situated right on the Little Miami Scenic Trail, Miranda's has become a beloved stop for families, bicyclists, canoeists, hikers, horseback riders, and lovers of delicious gourmet ice cream.

"We are pleased and proud to be so warmly welcomed by the people of Morrow."
— Olga and Anne

Giving Back

A Historic Setting

Yes—it's true! Miranda's is located in a charming historic bank building in the heart of Morrow, Ohio. We've preserved the original vault right in our dining room, creating a unique blend of history and hospitality that you won't find anywhere else.

Step inside and experience the character of a bygone era, complete with architectural details that tell the story of our town's heritage. Whether you're dining in the vault or relaxing on our spacious patio, every visit feels like a special occasion.
