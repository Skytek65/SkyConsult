Our Story

Two Sisters, One Sweet Dream

How a horseback ride through Morrow led to a gathering place for the whole community

A Discovery on Horseback

Miranda's Ice Cream Shoppe is owned and operated by sisters-in-law Olga and Anne.

Olga discovered the previously-owned Miranda's Old Time Ice Cream while horseback riding through Morrow. She and Anne decided it was the perfect location to open a gathering place for family and friends to meet, laugh, and enjoy gourmet ice cream.

Situated on the Little Miami Scenic Trail, Miranda's Ice Cream Shoppe serves families, bicyclists, canoeists, hikers, horseback riders and lovers of delicious gourmet ice cream.

Our Mission & Values

More than just ice cream—we're here to make a difference

Community First

We proudly serve families, bicyclists, canoeists, hikers, and horseback riders. Our electric bike charging stations and horse-friendly amenities ensure everyone feels welcome.

Giving Back

In the spirit of making a difference, Miranda's donates a portion of proceeds to organizations that help victims of domestic violence.

Historic Preservation

Located in a charming historic bank building, we've preserved the original vault in our dining room, blending history with hospitality.

Trail Connection

Situated directly on the Little Miami Scenic Trail, we're committed to being a rest stop and gathering place for outdoor enthusiasts.

Quality & Care

Home of the Unicone! We serve premium gourmet ice cream with generous portions, fresh ingredients, and the kind of care that only a family-owned business can provide.

Gathering Place

From birthday parties to wedding receptions, our Event Room and Patio are designed to host your most precious moments.
