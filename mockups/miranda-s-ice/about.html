Our Story

Two Sisters, One Sweet Dream

Discover how a chance horseback ride through Morrow led to the creation of your favorite neighborhood gathering spot.

How It All Began

Miranda's Ice Cream Shoppe is owned and operated by sisters-in-law Olga and Anne. The story began when Olga discovered the previously-owned Miranda's Old Time Ice Cream while horseback riding through the charming town of Morrow.

Immediately falling in love with the location and its potential, Olga and Anne decided it was the perfect spot to create something special—a gathering place where family and friends could meet, laugh, and enjoy premium gourmet ice cream together.

Situated on the Little Miami Scenic Trail, Miranda's has become a beloved stop for families, bicyclists, canoeists, hikers, horseback riders, and lovers of delicious gourmet ice cream.

Dine in a Vault

Yes—it's true! Miranda's is located in a charming historic bank building, and we've kept the original vault right in our dining room. Come experience a unique blend of history and hospitality that you won't find anywhere else.

Our spacious back patio offers the perfect setting for enjoying warm weather with an ice cream cone or a delicious meal. With ample seating, a pergola, fire pit, lawn games, and more, it's the ideal hangout for families, friends, and fun.

Rain or Shine, We've Got You Covered. When the weather doesn't cooperate, our cozy Event Room welcomes you indoors in comfort and style.

Rooted in Community

In the spirit of making a difference and giving back, Miranda's donates a portion of proceeds to organizations that help the victims of domestic violence. We believe that great ice cream should do more than just taste good—it should do good.
