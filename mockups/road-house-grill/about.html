Our Story

More than just a food truck—we're a family-owned culinary experience serving up unforgettable moments across Ohio.

From Dream to Reality

Road House Grill began with a simple belief: that great food has the power to bring people together. Founded by Billie Agnone, what started as a passion for cooking has grown into a beloved food trailer and catering service serving Warren, Clinton, Clermont, Greene, Clark, Montgomery, Hamilton Counties and beyond.

We specialize in handcrafted American comfort food made with love. Our slow-cooked pulled pork, creamy homemade mac and cheese, and fresh-baked buns have become local legends. Every dish is prepared with locally sourced beef and the finest ingredients, because we believe quality matters.

But we're more than just great food. We're about creating experiences. From intimate family gatherings to corporate events and weddings, we bring the flavor and the fun. Our famous murder mystery dinners have become a local favorite, combining delicious cuisine with interactive entertainment.
