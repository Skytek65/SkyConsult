Where Passion Meets the Plate

Road House Grill began with a simple dream: to serve handcrafted, gourmet meals that bring people together. What started as a passion project has grown into a beloved food trailer and full-service catering company serving Warren, Clinton, Clermont, Greene, Clark, Montgomery, Hamilton Counties and beyond.

Our founder, Billie Agnone, believed that great food should be accessible, memorable, and made with love. That belief drives everything we do—from selecting locally sourced beef to baking our fresh homemade buns daily.

Whether we're serving stuffed mushrooms at a murder mystery dinner, pulled pork at a corporate event, or burgers at a community festival, we bring the same dedication to quality and service that has defined us from day one.

Our Journey

The milestones that shaped Road House Grill into what it is today.

2014

The Beginning

Road House Grill was founded with a simple food trailer and a big dream: to serve incredible food with heart.

2016

Expanding Our Reach

Added full-service catering for weddings and corporate events, bringing our mobile kitchen to celebrations across the region.

2019

Murder Mystery Dinners

Launched our signature interactive dining experiences, combining great food with entertainment for unforgettable evenings.

2024

Today & Beyond

Continuing to serve Warren, Clinton, and surrounding counties with the same passion, now offering lunch boxes, drop-off catering, and food truck services.
