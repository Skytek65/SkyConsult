Our Story

A Culinary Journey Through Time

S.W. Clyborne Co. Provision & Spirits was born from a vision to create something truly extraordinary in Mason, Ohio. Our founders, inspired by the innovation of the Victorian era and the industrial revolution, set out to craft a dining experience unlike any other.

Every corner of our gastropub tells a story—from the hand-selected brass fixtures to the carefully curated provisions on your plate. We believe that dining should be an adventure, a journey that engages all your senses and leaves you with memories as lasting as the golden age that inspires us.
