Our Story

Bringing Mexico's Finest Flavors to Mason

El Trompo Mexican Grill was founded with a passion for authentic Mexican cuisine. Named after the traditional vertical spit used to make tacos al pastor, our restaurant brings the vibrant street food culture of Mexico to the heart of Mason, Ohio.

Every dish we serve is prepared with love, using time-honored family recipes passed down through generations. From our hand-pressed tortillas to our slow-marinated meats, we take pride in offering an authentic taste of Mexico in every bite.
