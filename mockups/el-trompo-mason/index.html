Our Story

Bringing Authentic
Mexican Flavors to Ohio

At El Trompo Mexican Grill, we believe that great food starts with fresh ingredients and authentic recipes passed down through generations. Our passion for Mexican cuisine drives everything we do, from hand-rolling our tortillas daily to slow-cooking our meats to perfection.

Located conveniently on Kings Mills Road in Mason, we've become a local favorite for families, professionals, and anyone craving a taste of Mexico. Whether you're joining us for a quick lunch, a family dinner, or takeout for home, we promise a dining experience that delights your senses.
