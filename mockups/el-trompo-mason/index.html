Our Story

Made with Passion,
Served with Love

El Trompo Mexican Grill brings authentic Mexican cuisine to the heart of Mason, Ohio. Our name "El Trompo" refers to the traditional vertical spit used to cook authentic al pastor meat – a centerpiece of our kitchen and a symbol of our commitment to tradition.

Every dish we serve is crafted with fresh, high-quality ingredients and time-honored recipes. From our hand-pressed tortillas to our house-made salsas, we take pride in offering an authentic Mexican dining experience that keeps our customers coming back for more.
