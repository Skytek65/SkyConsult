About Our Practice

Meet Dr. Daniel J. Vogt

Dr. Daniel J. Vogt has been serving the Loveland community with exceptional dental care. With a commitment to patient comfort and clinical excellence, Dr. Vogt provides comprehensive dental services in a warm, welcoming environment.

Our practice focuses on preventive care, helping families maintain healthy smiles for life. Whether you need a routine cleaning, restorative work, or cosmetic enhancements, you'll receive personalized attention and the highest quality treatment.

Our Services

Comprehensive Dental Care

From preventive maintenance to smile transformations, we offer a full range of dental services to meet your family's needs.

Preventive Care

Regular check-ups, professional cleanings, and dental exams to maintain optimal oral health and catch issues early.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Teeth whitening, veneers, and smile makeovers to help you achieve the confident, beautiful smile you deserve.

Restorative Care

Fillings, crowns, bridges, and implants to repair damaged teeth and restore your smile's function and appearance.

Emergency Dentistry

Prompt care for dental emergencies including toothaches, broken teeth, and other urgent dental needs.

Family Dentistry

Gentle, compassionate care for patients of all ages. From children's first visits to senior dental care.

Oral Surgery

Extractions, including wisdom teeth removal, performed with care and attention to your comfort and recovery.
