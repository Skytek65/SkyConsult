Our Story of
Authentic Turkish Cuisine

Truva Turkish Kitchen first opened its doors in Cincinnati in 2015, quickly becoming a local favorite for those seeking the true taste of Turkey. In 2021, we moved to our beautiful new Kenwood location, continuing our mission to share authentic Turkish and Mediterranean flavors with the community.

In March 2024, we proudly expanded to Mason, bringing a fresh and modern dining atmosphere to the northern Cincinnati area. Our Mason restaurant blends tradition with contemporary style, offering guests a rich menu that caters to every palate — from meat lovers to vegetarian guests.

Truva's Mason location features a private dining room for up to 40 guests — perfect for birthdays, business meetings, or family gatherings.
