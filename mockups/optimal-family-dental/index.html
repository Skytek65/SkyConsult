Meet Your Dentist

Dr. Meera Thunga, DDS

Dr. Thunga received her Doctor of Dental Surgery degree from New York University (NYU) in 2003. Prior to her studies at NYU, she completed her Bachelor of Dental Surgery and Internship from India. She has been practicing in the Cincinnati area since 2003, providing quality family, restorative and cosmetic dental care.

Dr. Thunga actively seeks out the newest technology, best materials and latest techniques available in the industry today. Her emphasis on patient comfort, along with staying up to date through Continuing Education programs, enables her to provide patients with the best quality of care in the field.
