About Our Practice

Committed to Your Family's Oral Health

Dr. Thunga received her Doctor of Dental Surgery degree from New York University (NYU) in 2003. Prior to her studies at NYU, she completed her Bachelor of Dental Surgery and Internship from India.

She has been practicing in the Cincinnati area since 2003, providing quality family, restorative and cosmetic dental care. Dr. Thunga actively seeks out the newest technology, best materials and latest techniques available in the industry today.

Her emphasis on patient comfort, as well as staying up to date with the latest advancements in dentistry through Continuing Education programs, enable her to provide patients with the best quality of care available in the field.

Our Services

Comprehensive Dental Care for Your Entire Family

From routine cleanings to advanced cosmetic procedures, we offer a full range of dental services to keep your smile healthy and beautiful.

Cleaning & Prevention

Regular cleanings, exams, and education on proper oral hygiene to prevent tooth decay and gum disease.

  • Professional Cleanings
  • Comprehensive Exams
  • Gum Disease Treatment

Cosmetic Dentistry

Enhance your smile's appearance with our range of cosmetic dental procedures for a confident, radiant smile.

  • Teeth Whitening
  • Porcelain Veneers
  • Dental Bonding

Restorative Dentistry

Restore damaged or missing teeth with our advanced restorative procedures for optimal function and aesthetics.

  • Dental Crowns & Bridges
  • Tooth-Colored Fillings
  • Root Canal Therapy

Fast Braces

Straighten your teeth faster with Fast Braces technology. Achieve a beautiful smile in less time than traditional braces.

  • Shorter Treatment Time
  • Comfortable Design

Pediatric Dentistry

Gentle, friendly dental care for children. We create positive dental experiences to set the foundation for lifelong oral health.

  • Child-Friendly Environment
  • Gentle Treatment Approach

Sleep Apnea Treatment

Having trouble sleeping? We offer custom oral appliances to help you breathe better and sleep soundly through the night.

  • Custom Oral Appliances
  • Non-Invasive Solution
