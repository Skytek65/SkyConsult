About Us

Delicious Food, Exceptional Service

Road House Grill is your premier mobile catering and food truck service, bringing mouthwatering cuisine directly to your events, parties, and gatherings.

🚚

Why Choose Road House Grill?

We're more than just a food truck – we're your complete catering solution. As a women-owned business, we bring passion, dedication, and culinary excellence to every event we serve.

From intimate family gatherings to large corporate events, our mobile kitchen delivers fresh, delicious meals that will have your guests raving. We serve Warren, Clinton, Clermont, Greene, Clark, Montgomery, and Hamilton Counties.

  • Fresh, locally-sourced ingredients
  • Customizable menus for any event
  • Professional, friendly staff
  • Full-service catering setup
  • Flexible booking options
