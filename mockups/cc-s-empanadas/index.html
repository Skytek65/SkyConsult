Our Story

Bringing Venezuela to Loveland

At CC's Empanadas, we're passionate about sharing the authentic flavors of Venezuela with our community. Every empanada is handcrafted using traditional recipes passed down through generations, using only the freshest ingredients.

From our signature empanadas to our delicious arepas, each bite transports you to the streets of Caracas. We believe in quality, authenticity, and bringing people together through the love of good food.
