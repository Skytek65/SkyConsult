Our Story

From Humble Beginnings to Loveland's Favorite

The Journey

Passion Meets Smoke

Pig Candy BBQ began with a simple mission: bring authentic, slow-smoked barbecue to Loveland. What started as a passion project has grown into a local institution, serving up creative bold flavors that keep our community coming back for more.

We believe great BBQ takes time. That's why we start early every day, tending to our smokers and ensuring every brisket, rib, and pulled pork serving meets our high standards. No shortcuts, no compromises—just honest food made with care.
