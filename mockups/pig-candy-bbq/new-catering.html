The Food

Smoked Low & Slow

Every catering order features our signature meats, smoked fresh daily in our Loveland pit. Choose from brisket, pulled pork, smoked turkey, sausage, and more.

Complement your meats with scratch-made sides including smoked mac and cheese, collard greens, fresh salad, cornbread, and our famous banana pudding. All sauces and buns are provided.

Service

We Deliver & Set Up

The Pig Candy van brings the feast directly to your venue. We offer flexible service options from simple drop-off delivery to full-service setup with chafing dishes, serving utensils, and on-site staff.

Whether your event is indoors or outdoors, we handle the heavy lifting so you can focus on your guests. Advance booking recommended.
