Common Questions

What People Ask

What is Your Most Popular Item?

Our Brisket and Pulled Pork sandwiches are both fan favorites.

How Much Advanced Notice is Needed for Catering?

We have set packages that can be placed online for parties of 12 or 24 with just 5 days notice. For all other orders, a week's notice is ideal.

Are You Gluten Free?

All of our meats and most of our sides and desserts are Gluten Free. We have dedicated equipment for Gluten and Non-Gluten Items. We do take it seriously and to our knowledge we haven't had a single issue. That being said, there is Gluten in the kitchen and the possibility of cross contamination exists.

Do You Have Vegetarian Options?

For vegetarians we have our Vegan Goetta, all sides (Other than Cowboy and Green Beans).

Where is Parking?

At Kellogg there is parking on the side of the building as well as behind the building with signs directing where to park. At Loveland there are two designated lots and street parking.

Do You Serve Alcohol?

Yes, we serve beer, wine, hard seltzers, and mimosas.

Do You Take Reservations?

No, we do not hold reservations. However, feel free to call before you come and ask about open seating.

Are You Handicap Accessible?

We have ramps and handicap accessible bathrooms available at both locations.
