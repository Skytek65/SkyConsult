Our Story

Serving Loveland & Cincinnati

Located in Loveland, Ohio, Pig Candy BBQ brings creative bold flavors to the Cincinnati area. We specialize in slow-smoked meats and house-made sauces, served up in a casual, welcoming atmosphere. Whether you're joining us for a quick lunch or feeding a crowd, we're passionate about delivering authentic BBQ with every bite.
