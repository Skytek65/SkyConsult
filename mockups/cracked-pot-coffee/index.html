Our Story

Celebrating Our
Beautiful Flaws

The Cracked Pot is a family owned and operated cafe that offers a wide variety of crepes and coffee. Our name was inspired by an old fable about a person who carries two pots down to a stream to collect water.

While one pot carries water perfectly, the other has a crack, leaking water along the path. Though this cracked pot doesn't carry the full portion as intended, each time it's carried down the path, it waters the plants, bringing life to the edge of the path.

Here at The Cracked Pot, we celebrate the idea that we all have flaws, but that is what makes us unique and beautiful!
