Our Story

Bringing the Caribbean
to Middletown, Ohio

At Umbrella Restaurant & Lounge, we're passionate about sharing the rich, vibrant flavors of Dominican cuisine with our community. Our family recipes have been passed down through generations, featuring authentic dishes like our signature mofongo, crispy empanadas, and fresh Caribbean seafood.

Whether you're craving a taste of home or looking to explore new flavors, our warm, family-friendly atmosphere and handcrafted cocktails make every visit a celebration. Come experience why we're rated 5 stars by our guests!
