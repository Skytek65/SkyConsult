OUR STORY

More Than a Restaurant,
It's an Experience

El Trompo Mexican Grill was born from a passion for authentic Mexican cuisine and a desire to create memorable dining experiences. Our name "El Trompo" refers to the traditional vertical spit used to cook the beloved tacos al pastor – a technique passed down through generations.

With multiple locations across the Cincinnati area including Mason, West Chester, Monroe, and Colerain, we've become the go-to destination for families, friends, and food lovers seeking bold flavors in a vibrant atmosphere.
