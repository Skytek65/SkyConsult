Our Story

The Art of Korean Cuisine

At Dolsot Bistro, we bring the rich culinary traditions of Korea to Mason, Ohio. Our name comes from the "dolsot" — the traditional Korean stone pot that brings dishes to sizzling perfection at your table.

Every dish is prepared with authentic ingredients and time-honored recipes, ensuring an experience that honors Korean culinary heritage while embracing modern presentation.
