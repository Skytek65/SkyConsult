"The Lebanese Food Embassy"

At Phoenician Taverna, we bring the rich culinary traditions of Lebanon to Mason, Ohio. Every dish on our menu is prepared entirely from scratch in-house, using authentic recipes passed down through generations.

From our signature hummus and freshly baked flatbreads to our perfectly grilled kebabs and traditional Lebanese desserts, we take pride in offering an authentic dining experience that transports you to the Mediterranean coast.
