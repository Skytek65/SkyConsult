Our Story

A Taste of Lebanon
in Mason, Ohio

Phoenician Taverna brings the rich culinary heritage of Lebanon to the heart of Mason. Our name honors the ancient Phoenicians, master traders who spread Mediterranean culture across the ancient world.

Every dish on our menu is prepared entirely from scratch in-house, using traditional recipes passed down through generations. From our velvety hummus to our perfectly spiced kibbeh, authenticity is our signature ingredient.

We are proud to be Mason's Lebanese Food Embassy – a place where the warmth of Lebanese hospitality meets the finest Mediterranean cuisine.

Our Menu

Authentic Lebanese Favorites

Every dish is crafted from scratch using traditional Lebanese recipes and the freshest ingredients.

Cold Mezza (Vegetarian)

Hummus

$8.00

Chickpeas pureed with tahini, lemon and garlic. Sprinkled with extra virgin olive oil.

Baba Ghannouge

$9.00

Smoked roasted eggplants pureed with tahini, labneh, fresh lemon and garlic. Sprinkled with EV olive oil and pomegranate seeds when in season.

Dip Trio

$10.50

Three dips combination of Hummus, Baba Ghannouge, and Mama Ghannouge (roasted beets dip).

Warak Inab Bil Zeit

$9.50

Vegetarian grape leaves. Rolled vine leaves with rice, chickpeas, tomatoes, parsley and herbs in lemon and extra virgin olive oil.

Hot Mezza (Vegetarian)

Falafel

$9.50

Chickpea fritters. Coarsely ground chickpeas with leeks, garlic, cilantro, parsley, spices and herbs. Served with tomatoes, radishes, scallions and tahini sauce.

Kibbeh Lakteen

$10.50

Vegan kibbeh! The shell is burghul and pumpkin filled with walnuts, onions and pomegranate reduction.

Main Dishes

Mashawi M'shakkal

$28.00

Mixed kebab. Grilled tender cubes of chicken, lamb and kafta, served with grilled vegetables, basmati rice, garlic whip and our salsa harra.

Mouzat

$33.00

Braised lamb shank. Lamb shank braised in tomato sauce, served with crushed wheat pilaf.

Shawarma Djaj

Vertical rotisserie chicken. Marinated chicken breasts slowly cooked on the vertical rotisserie, served with onion-sumac-parsley, basmati rice and garlic whip.

Salmon Meshwi
