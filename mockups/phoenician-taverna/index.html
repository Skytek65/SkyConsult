Our Story

A Taste of Lebanon
in Mason, Ohio

At Phoenician Taverna, we bring the authentic flavors of Lebanon to your table. Every dish is prepared from scratch using traditional recipes passed down through generations, featuring fresh ingredients and time-honored cooking techniques.

From our signature hummus and falafel to our succulent kebabs and house-made sausages, each plate tells a story of Lebanese culinary heritage. Our commitment to quality and authenticity has made us the Lebanese Food Embassy of Mason.
