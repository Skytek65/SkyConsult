Our Story

A Taste of Lebanon
in the Heart of Mason

Phoenician Taverna is more than just a restaurant—it's a journey to the Mediterranean. Our recipes have been passed down through generations, preserving the authentic flavors of Lebanese cuisine.

Every dish on our menu is executed entirely from scratch in-house. From our freshly baked pita bread to our signature hummus and tender kebabs, we use only the finest ingredients to bring you an authentic taste of Lebanon.

Our commitment to excellence has earned us over 800 five-star reviews from our beloved community in Mason and beyond. We invite you to experience why we're known as the "Lebanese Food Embassy."
