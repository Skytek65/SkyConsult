Our Story

A Historic Gem in
Downtown Loveland

The Works radiates with restored charm and character, creating a warm and inviting atmosphere where families and friends can enjoy themselves. Our goal and mission is to continue bringing our guests a memorable dining experience that is unique and delicious.

Located in a historic building dating back to 1905, once home to the Loveland Electric Light and Power Company, we've transformed this beautiful space into a beloved dining destination. We've recently brought back "Live Music" at The Works plus installed new light fixtures to enhance your experience.
