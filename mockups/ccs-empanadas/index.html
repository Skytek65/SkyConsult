Our Story

Bringing Latin Tradition
to Loveland, Ohio

At CC's Empanadas, we believe that great food comes from the heart. Our empanadas are made using time-honored recipes passed down through generations, bringing the authentic taste of South America right to your neighborhood.

Every empanada is handcrafted with love, using only the freshest ingredients and traditional techniques. From our flaky, golden crusts to our savory, perfectly seasoned fillings, we put our passion into every bite.
