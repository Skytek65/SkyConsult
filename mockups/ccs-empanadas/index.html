Our Story

Bringing Venezuela to Ohio

CC's Empanadas was born from a simple dream: to share the authentic flavors of Venezuela with our community in Loveland. Every empanada we make carries the warmth of our heritage and the love of homemade cooking.

Our dough is made fresh daily using traditional corn flour, and each filling is prepared with recipes passed down through generations. From the first bite, you'll taste the difference that authenticity makes.
