Our Story

Bringing Venezuela to Loveland

CC's Empanadas was founded with a simple mission: to share the authentic flavors of Venezuela with our community in Loveland, Ohio. Every empanada we make is crafted with traditional recipes passed down through generations, using the finest ingredients and the same care you'd find in a Venezuelan home kitchen.

Our signature corn dough (harina pan) is made fresh daily, and each empanada is hand-folded to ensure the perfect crispy exterior and delicious filling. Whether you're craving the classic Pabellon or trying something new, we guarantee an authentic taste experience.
