Our Story

Bringing Venezuela to Loveland

At CC's Empanadas, we're passionate about sharing the authentic flavors of Venezuela with our Cincinnati community. Every empanada is handcrafted using traditional recipes passed down through generations.

Our cornmeal dough is made fresh daily, creating that signature super-crunchy exterior that our customers rave about. We stuff each empanada with generous portions of flavorful fillings - from classic Pabellón to creative Cincinnati-inspired combinations like our Lentil & Goetta empanada.
