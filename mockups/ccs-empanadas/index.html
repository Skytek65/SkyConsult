Our Story

Authentic Recipes,
Local Heart

CC's Empanadas was born from a passion for authentic Latin American cuisine and a dream to share family recipes with the Loveland community.

Every empanada is handcrafted fresh daily using time-honored techniques passed down through generations. We use only the finest ingredients, from premium meats to locally sourced vegetables when possible.

What started as a small kitchen experiment has grown into a beloved local spot where families, friends, and food lovers gather to enjoy a taste of Latin America right here in Ohio.
