Our Story

Bringing Venezuela to Loveland

Every empanada begins with 100% gluten-free cornmeal dough, hand-filled with your choice of 18 authentic flavors, then fried until perfectly golden and crispy. It's the taste of Venezuela in every bite.

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers!

Are your empanadas really gluten-free?

Yes! All our empanadas are made with 100% cornmeal dough, making them naturally gluten-free. However, please note that we do fry in shared oil.

Do you have vegan options?

Absolutely! We offer several vegan options including our Lentils empanada, Domino (black beans & cheese), and Vegan Bean arepa. Just ask our staff for recommendations!

Can I order for a large group or event?

Yes! We accept catering orders for parties and events. Please call us at (513) 239-6700 at least 24 hours in advance for large orders.

What's the difference between empanadas and arepas?

Empanadas are crispy, fried cornmeal pockets filled with savory ingredients. Arepas are thicker cornmeal cakes that are split open and stuffed like sandwiches. Both are delicious!
