From Our Garden to Your Plate

Our menu evolves with the growing season. In spring, expect tender greens and asparagus; summer brings tomatoes and berries; fall offers squash and root vegetables; winter features hearty greens and preserved harvests. We proudly source eggs from pasture-raised hens, grass-fed meats from regional ranchers, and organic produce from our own gardens whenever possible. Ask your server about today's garden specials.
