Our Story

Fresh to Morrow Cafe is a farm-to-table restaurant near Cincinnati, located in the heart of Morrow, Ohio. We serve guests from nearby communities like Lebanon, Loveland, Maineville, and Mason, as well as visitors passing through Southwest Ohio.

Because we believe food should stay connected to the land, we grow much of our own produce and work directly with local farmers. As a result, our menu changes with the seasons and reflects what is fresh right now.

Whether you are stopping in for breakfast, meeting friends for lunch, or looking for a place that values real food and community, Fresh to Morrow offers a welcoming space to slow down and eat well.

Our Values

Every decision we make is guided by our commitment to quality, community, and sustainability.

🌱

Farm to Table

We grow much of our own produce right here in Morrow. What we don't grow ourselves, we source directly from local farmers who share our values. This means your meal starts with what is in season, not what ships well.

🤝

Community First

Located near the Little Miami Scenic Trail, we're a favorite stop for cyclists and visitors. But we stay deeply rooted in Morrow, creating space for neighbors to connect over live music, seasonal events, and shared meals.

🍃

Seasonal & Fresh

Our menu changes with the seasons because that's when ingredients taste best. No frozen shortcuts or out-of-season compromises—just thoughtful, fresh food that feels straight from the garden.
