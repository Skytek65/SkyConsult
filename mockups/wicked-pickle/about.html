About Us

Our Location

Heart of Downtown Loveland

We are located along the Little Miami Bike trail located in the heart of downtown Loveland, Ohio. The outdoor seating offers a wonderful shaded patio that's perfect any day.

Whether you're stopping by during your bike ride or looking for a relaxing spot to enjoy great food and drinks, our location offers the perfect setting for any occasion.

Our Bar & Grill

Delicious sandwiches, empanadas, slushies & appetizers. Updated menu, but still offering some all-time favorite items including Wicked Lemonade.
