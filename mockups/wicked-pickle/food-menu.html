Fresh & Flavorful Delicious Food

From our signature pickles to hearty sandwiches and fresh salads, every dish is crafted with passion and served with a smile at our Loveland location.

Our Menu

At The Wicked Pickle, we believe in serving up comfort food with a twist. Our menu features locally-sourced ingredients, house-made pickles, and recipes that have been perfected over years of love and experimentation.

Whether you're craving a loaded sandwich, a fresh salad, or just want to snack on our famous pickles, we've got something to satisfy every appetite.
