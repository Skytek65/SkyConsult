A Few Words About Us

Serving The Best Sandwiches, Empanadas, & Shareables Around! We offer delicious sandwiches, empanadas, slushies, and appetizers. Updated menu, but still offering some all-time favorite items including Wicked Lemonade. We have a full bar with ever changing ice cold beer on draft along with amazing craft beers and as always your normal favorites!

We open daily at 11AM to serve you with amazing house made foods and the coldest beer along the Loveland bike trail. We have an inviting atmosphere and a friendly staff ready to help you have the most enjoyable eating and drinking experience!
