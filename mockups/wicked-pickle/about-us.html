About Us

Located along the Little Miami Bike trail in the heart of downtown Loveland, Ohio.

A Few Words About Us

Wicked Good Food

We are located along the Little Miami Bike trail located in the heart of downtown Loveland, Ohio. The outdoor seating offers a wonderful shaded patio that's perfect any day.

We open daily at 11AM to serve you with amazing house made foods and the coldest beer along the Loveland bike trail. We have an inviting atmosphere and a friendly staff ready to help you have the most enjoyable eating and drinking experience!

What We Serve

Our Bar & Grill

Serving the best sandwiches, empanadas, and shareables around.

House Made Favorites

Delicious sandwiches, empanadas, slushies, and appetizers. Updated menu, but still offering some all-time favorite items including Wicked Lemonade.

Two Full Bars

Inside or outside, we have 2 bar locations fully stocked with the best beer and cocktails around. We have a full bar with ever changing ice cold beer on draft along with amazing craft beers and as always your normal favorites.

Trailside Amenities

Direct access from the Little Miami Bike Trail with plenty of bike parking. Free WiFi for everyone—ask for the password to any staff member.
