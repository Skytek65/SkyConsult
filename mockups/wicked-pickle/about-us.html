About Us

Located along the Little Miami Bike Trail in the heart of downtown Loveland, Ohio

Our Bar & Grill

Serving the Best Sandwiches, Empanadas, & Shareables Around

We serve delicious sandwiches, empanadas, slushies, and appetizers. Our updated menu still offers all-time favorite items including our signature Wicked Lemonade.

We feature a full bar with ever-changing ice cold beer on draft, amazing craft beers, and as always, your normal favorites!

A Few Words About Us

An Inviting Atmosphere Along the Loveland Bike Trail

We open daily at 11AM to serve you with amazing house-made foods and the coldest beer along the Loveland Bike Trail. Our outdoor seating offers a wonderful shaded patio that's perfect any day.

We have an inviting atmosphere and a friendly staff ready to help you have the most enjoyable eating and drinking experience!

  • Free WiFi for everyone (ask any staff for the password)
  • Shaded outdoor patio seating
  • Family-friendly, energetic environment

Our Core Values

The Wicked Pickle values its customers. We do our best to provide amazing food, a fun and energetic atmosphere that's great for the whole family, a great drink selection, and many other fun activities!
