About The Wicked Pickle

Trailside Location

Our Story

We are located along the Little Miami Bike trail in the heart of downtown Loveland, Ohio. The outdoor seating offers a wonderful shaded patio that's perfect for any day.

We open daily at 11AM to serve you with amazing house-made foods and the coldest beer along the Loveland bike trail. We have an inviting atmosphere and a friendly staff ready to help you have the most enjoyable eating and drinking experience!
