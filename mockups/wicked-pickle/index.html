Wicked Good Food

Our delicious story starts along the Little Miami Scenic Trail.

We wanted a name that invoked a Key West feel. The name came partly from the popular restaurant in Key West, The Wicked Lick, and partly from cyclists on the Little Miami Scenic Trail that eat pickles and drink pickle juice when they stop in Loveland.

The outdoor seating offers a wonderful shaded area that's perfect any day. We open daily at 11 AM to serve you with amazing house-made foods and the coldest beer along the Loveland bike trail.
