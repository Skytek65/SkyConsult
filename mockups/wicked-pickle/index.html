Our Delicious Story

Wicked Good Food

We are located along the Little Miami Bike trail, in the heart of downtown Loveland, Ohio. The outdoor seating offers a wonderful shaded area that's perfect any day.

We wanted a name that invoked a Key West feel. The name came partly from the popular restaurant in Key West, The Wicked Lick, and partly from cyclists on the Little Miami Scenic Trail that eat pickles and drink pickle juice when they stop in Loveland.
