Welcome To The Wicked Pickle

Located along the Little Miami Bike Trail in the heart of downtown Loveland, Ohio. Enjoy wicked good food, craft cocktails, and live music in our shaded outdoor patio.

Explore Menu View Events

Wicked Good Food

We wanted a name that invoked a Key West feel. The name came partly from the popular restaurant in Key West, The Wicked Lick, and partly from cyclists on the Little Miami Scenic Trail that eat pickles and drink pickle juice when they stop in Loveland.

"The outdoor seating offers a wonderful shaded area that's perfect any day. Come enjoy one of our delicious cocktails!"

Serving the best sandwiches, empanadas, and shareables around. All meats are served with house pickles. We have a full bar with ever-changing ice cold beer on draft along with amazing craft beers.
