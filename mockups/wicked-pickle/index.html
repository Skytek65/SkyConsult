Our Delicious Story

Wicked Good Food

We wanted a name that invoked a Key West feel. The name came partly from the popular restaurant in Key West, The Wicked Lick, and partly from cyclists on the Little Miami Scenic Trail that eat pickles and drink pickle juice when they stop in Loveland.

We are located along the Little Miami Bike Trail in the heart of downtown Loveland, Ohio. Our outdoor seating offers a wonderful shaded patio that's perfect any day. We open daily at 11 AM to serve you with amazing house-made foods and the coldest beer along the trail.
