Wicked Good Food

Our Delicious Story — We wanted a name that invoked a Key West feel. The name came partly from the popular restaurant in Key West, The Wicked Lick, and partly from cyclists on the Little Miami Scenic Trail that eat pickles and drink pickle juice when they stop in Loveland.

We are located along the Little Miami Bike Trail, in the heart of downtown Loveland, Ohio. The outdoor seating offers a wonderful shaded area that's perfect any day.

We have a full bar with ever-changing ice cold beer on draft along with amazing craft beers and as always your normal favorites! We open daily at 11 AM to serve you with amazing house-made foods and the coldest beer along the Loveland bike trail.
