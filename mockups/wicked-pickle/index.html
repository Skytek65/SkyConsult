Welcome to The Wicked Pickle

Located along the Little Miami Bike Trail in the heart of downtown Loveland, Ohio. Enjoy wicked good food, cold beer, and incredible cocktails with a Key West vibe.

Explore Menu Our Story

Wicked Good Food

We wanted a name that invoked a Key West feel. The name came partly from the popular restaurant in Key West, The Wicked Lick, and partly from cyclists on the Little Miami Scenic Trail that eat pickles and drink pickle juice when they stop in Loveland.

Our outdoor seating offers a wonderful shaded patio that's perfect any day. We have a full bar with ever-changing ice cold beer on draft along with amazing craft beers and your normal favorites.

We open daily at 11 AM to serve you with amazing house-made foods and the coldest beer along the Loveland bike trail.
