About Us

A Deerfield Tradition Since 1963

Nestled at the heart of Deerfield on Route 14, Circle Restaurant has been serving up hearty, homestyle meals that bring families together. We're known throughout Northeast Ohio for our massive pancakes that hang over the plate, our famous "He Man" breakfast, and our commitment to good old-fashioned American comfort food.

Whether you're starting your day with our all-day breakfast, enjoying a leisurely lunch with friends, or bringing the family for dinner, you'll find the same warm welcome and generous portions that have made us a local institution for over six decades.
