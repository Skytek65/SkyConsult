About Us

Where Craft Meets Comfort

Bourbon's Kitchen takes the classic American pub experience to the next level with its modern, sleek atmosphere and exceptional menu. We're not just a restaurant—we're a destination for bourbon enthusiasts, craft cocktail lovers, and anyone who appreciates elevated comfort food.

From our signature Chicken & Waffles to our Grippo's-encrusted burgers and expertly crafted Smoked Old Fashioneds, every dish and drink is designed to delight. With over 150 unique bourbons and a rotating selection of craft beers on tap, there's always something new to discover.
