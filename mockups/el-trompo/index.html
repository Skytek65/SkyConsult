Our Story

Authentic Mexican Flavors in the Heart of Mason

At El Trompo Mexican Grill, we bring the vibrant flavors and warm hospitality of Mexico to Mason, Ohio. Our name comes from the traditional vertical rotisserie called "trompo" used to make our signature Tacos Al Pastor – marinated pork stacked high and carved fresh to order.

Every dish we serve is made with authentic recipes passed down through generations. From our handmade tortillas and fresh guacamole to our slow-cooked meats and house-made salsas, we use only the finest ingredients to create an unforgettable dining experience.
