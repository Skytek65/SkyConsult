Our Story

Bringing the Heart of Mexico to Mason

El Trompo Mexican Grill was founded in 2015 with a simple mission: to share the authentic flavors and warm hospitality of Mexico with our Mason community. Named after the traditional vertical spit used to cook al pastor meat, our restaurant honors the time-honored cooking techniques passed down through generations.

Every dish we serve is crafted with fresh, high-quality ingredients sourced from local suppliers whenever possible. Our recipes come from family traditions, and our passion for authentic Mexican cuisine shines through in every bite. From our handmade tortillas to our slow-cooked carnitas, we're dedicated to bringing you a taste of Mexico right here in Ohio.
