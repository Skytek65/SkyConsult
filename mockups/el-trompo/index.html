At El Trompo Mexican Grill, we bring the vibrant flavors of Mexico to Mason, Ohio. Our recipes have been passed down through generations, each dish crafted with the same love and dedication that our abuela put into her cooking.

From our signature trompo-style tacos to our hand-made tortillas, every ingredient is carefully selected for quality and authenticity. We grind our own corn, slow-cook our meats, and create salsas that range from mild to "call the fire department."

House Specialties

What We're Famous For

Al Pastor Trompo

Our signature dish! Pork marinated for 24 hours in a blend of guajillo chiles, achiote, and pineapple. Cooked on our vertical trompo rotisserie and shaved fresh for each order. Served with pineapple, cilantro, onions, and our secret salsa verde.

Trompo Style 24hr Marinated Spicy & Sweet

Mole Poblano

A rich, complex sauce made from over 20 ingredients including chocolate, chilies, nuts, and spices. Served over tender chicken breast. This traditional dish from Puebla takes hours to prepare but seconds to fall in love with.

Traditional Recipe 20+ Ingredients All-Day Prep

Carne Asada

Premium skirt steak marinated in citrus and Mexican spices, then grilled to perfection over an open flame. Served with roasted jalapeños, nopales, fresh guacamole, and hand-made tortillas. A carnivore's dream come true.

Premium Steak Open Flame Citrus Marinade

El Trompo Special

The ultimate feast! A sizzling combination of al pastor, carne asada, and grilled chicken served on a hot skillet with peppers and onions. Includes rice, beans, guacamole, sour cream, pico de gallo, and 6 tortillas. Perfect for sharing!
