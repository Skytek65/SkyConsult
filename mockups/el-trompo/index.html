Our Story

A Vibrant Culinary Oasis in Mason

El Trompo Mexican Grill is a vibrant culinary destination nestled in the heart of Mason, Ohio. Located at 5271 Kings Mills Rd, this lively establishment has become celebrated for its delicious, fresh Mexican cuisine and an inviting atmosphere that keeps guests coming back.

Diners consistently rave about our superb shrimp dishes and expertly crafted margaritas. Our attentive staff, including star servers like David, Marianna, and Patricia, enhance every experience with prompt service and warm hospitality. The colorful decor creates a festive vibe perfect for casual gatherings or special celebrations.
