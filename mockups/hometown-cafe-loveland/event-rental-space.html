The Space

Intimate & Flexible

The main dining room can seat up to 25 people. Your evening event rental includes use of the patio, which accommodates up to 32 guests.

Talk to us if you'd like to bring in additional or different seating. Please note that no Hometown café or market items will be available for purchase during your evening event.
