Evening Rental Details

🕐

Hours

Access to the full space from 4pm to 11pm. Set up and tear down must be completed between these hours.

🪑

Capacity

Tables and chairs to seat up to 25 in the first floor dining room and 32 on the patio. Talk to us if you'd like to bring in additional or different seating.

🛒

Exclusive Use

No Hometown café or market items will be available for purchase during your evening event.

📝

Booking & Cancellation

Rental fee is due in full to reserve an evening date. 30-day cancellation notice required for full refund; 50% refund available for later cancellations.

Dining Options

Evening Catering Details

We can provide catering for your event, or you are welcome to have the event catered by someone else or bring your own food.

If kitchen access is required, there is a kitchen rental fee of $100 for the evening.
