Hours & Access

The evening rental rate includes access to the full space from 4pm to 11pm. Set up and tear down must be completed between these hours.

Seating Capacity

Hometown has tables and chairs to seat up to 25 in the first floor dining room and 32 on the patio. Talk to us if you'd like to bring in additional or different seating.

Policies

No Hometown café or market items will be available for purchase during your evening event.

Cancellation

30-day cancellation notice required for full refund, 50% refund available for later cancellations.

Catering Choices

We can provide catering for your event. You are welcome to have the event catered by someone else or bring your own food.

Kitchen Access

If kitchen access is required, there is a kitchen rental fee of $100 for the evening. Learn more about our kitchen rental.

Main Dining Room

Our first floor dining room provides an intimate setting with seating for up to 25 guests.

Outdoor Patio

Your evening event rental includes use of the patio, offering additional seating for up to 32 guests.
