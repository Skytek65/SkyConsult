The Space

Dining Room & Patio

Your evening rental includes access to the full space from 4pm to 11pm. Set up and tear down must be completed between these hours.

25 First Floor Dining Room
32 Patio Seating

Hometown has tables and chairs to seat up to 25 in the first floor dining room and 32 on the patio. Talk to us if you'd like to bring in additional or different seating.
