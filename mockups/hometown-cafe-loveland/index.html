Our Story

Rooted in Loveland

Hometown Cafe is the perfect spot for a relaxing break along the Little Miami Scenic Trail. Savor coffee and espresso brewed with locally roasted Deeper Roots beans. Enjoy made-to-order breakfast items and real fruit and veggie smoothies.

The cafe is stocked with local treats, snacks and products like cookies, candy, lotion, and energy bars & bites.
