Our Story

Your Home on the Trail

Nestled along the Little Miami Scenic Trail in historic downtown Loveland, Hometown Cafe is the perfect spot for a relaxing break. Savor coffee and espresso brewed with locally roasted Deeper Roots beans. Enjoy a made-to-order breakfast item. Refuel with a real fruit and veggie smoothie.

The cafe is stocked with local treats, snacks and products like cookies, candy, lotion, and energy bars & bites.
