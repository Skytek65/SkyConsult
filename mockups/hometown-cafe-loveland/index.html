A Home on the Trail

We are located in historic downtown Loveland, right along the Little Miami Scenic Trail. Savor coffee and espresso brewed with locally roasted Deeper Roots beans. Enjoy a made-to-order breakfast item, or refuel with a real fruit and veggie smoothie.

The cafe is stocked with local treats, snacks and products like cookies, candy, lotion, and energy bars & bites.
