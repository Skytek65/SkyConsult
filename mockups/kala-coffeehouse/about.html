About Kala

Drink Good. Do Good.

We chose our name because we are passionate about being a force for good in our community, in more ways than one.

Our Story

A good cup with good company for a good cause.

We are passionate about coffee and about people, so at Kala Coffeehouse, we bring those together. We offer a great cup of coffee in a friendly and welcoming environment.

Located in Mason, Ohio, we've built our coffeehouse on the belief that every cup should matter—not just for the person drinking it, but for the community that makes it possible.

From our carefully sourced beans to our commitment to local causes, every aspect of Kala is designed to create positive impact, one sip at a time.

We're Glad You Asked!

What makes Kala different? It comes down to three essential commitments.

01

Our Ingredients

We are committed to the best ingredients because if you want to be at your best, you need the best fuel available. That is why we choose minimally processed, locally sourced, carefully crafted food and drink.

02

Our Purpose

At Kala it's about more than a cup of coffee. It's about our community. We are from Warren County and for Warren County. That is why a portion of profits always goes to support local causes.

03

Our Atmosphere

We are committed to hospitality. Whether you are looking for a place to relax with a great book, gather with friends, meet a client, or catch up on work, come make yourself at home. Leave the dishes to us!

Fighting for Better Futures

One of the causes closest to our heart is the kids of Warren County and beyond. We seek to use resources to help children who are neglected, forgotten or overlooked. By choosing Kala, you are choosing to join in the fight for better futures for Warren County kids.
