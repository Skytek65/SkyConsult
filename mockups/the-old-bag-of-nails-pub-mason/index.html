Where good food meets great company

A Mason Tradition

The Old Bag of Nails Pub has been serving the Mason community with hearty pub fare and a welcoming atmosphere that feels like home. Our name honors the traditional Irish saying, representing a place where friends gather and memories are made.

We're more than just a restaurant – we're your neighborhood pub where locals come to unwind, celebrate, and enjoy the simple pleasures of great food and good company.

Whether you're stopping by for our famous fish and chips, grabbing a drink after work, or bringing the family for dinner, you'll always find a warm welcome at The Old Bag of Nails.
