Our Story

A Lebanon Institution Since 1953

For over seven decades, Houston Inn has been the gathering place for families, friends, and food lovers throughout Warren County. What started as a humble roadside eatery on Route 42 has become a legendary destination for those seeking authentic American cuisine in a warm, welcoming atmosphere.

Our rustic dining room, friendly service, and time-honored recipes have made us a beloved tradition for generations. From our famous frog legs that "taste just like chicken" to our legendary 54-item salad bar, every dish tells a story of culinary excellence and family pride.
