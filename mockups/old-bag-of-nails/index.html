Our Story

From Hardware Store to Neighborhood Icon

In 1996, we converted an old hardware store into something special—a neighborhood pub where friends gather, stories flow, and the fish & chips are always crispy.

The name "Old Bag of Nails" pays homage to the building's history while representing the authentic, no-frills pub experience we deliver every day.

For over 28 years, we've been serving Mason and the surrounding communities with scratch-made British and American pub favorites, ice-cold beers, and warm hospitality that keeps folks coming back.
