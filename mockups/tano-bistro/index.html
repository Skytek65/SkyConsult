Our Philosophy

Seasonally Fresh.
Naturally Lighter.

At Tano Bistro, we believe that delicious food deserves equally impressive companions. Our contemporary seasonally-inspired menu is simply crafted, full of flavor, and naturally lighter.

From our intimate dining space nestled in the heart of Loveland to our stunning rooftop patio, every detail is designed to elevate your dining experience. Our cocktails are exquisite and garnished like pieces of art, while our wine list features carefully curated new and old-world producers.
