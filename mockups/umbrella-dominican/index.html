About Us

Bringing the Heart of the Dominican Republic to Ohio

At Umbrella Dominican Restaurant, we believe food is more than sustenance—it's a celebration of culture, family, and tradition. Every dish we serve carries the warmth and soul of Dominican home cooking.

Our recipes have been passed down through generations, from the bustling streets of Santo Domingo to your plate here in Middletown. We use only the freshest ingredients, traditional spices, and time-honored cooking techniques to bring you an authentic taste of the Caribbean.

Traditional Dominican Favorites

Discover the rich flavors of Dominican cuisine. Each dish is prepared with authentic recipes and the finest ingredients.

Popular

La Bandera Dominicana

The national dish! White rice, stewed red beans, your choice of meat (chicken, beef, or pork), with salad and sweet plantains.

$14.99
Chef's Pick

Pernil Asado

Tender, slow-roasted pork shoulder marinated in garlic, oregano, and citrus. Fall-off-the-bone goodness served with rice and beans.

$16.99

Mofongo

Fried green plantains mashed with garlic, chicharrón, and olive oil. Served with your choice of chicken, shrimp, or pork in creole sauce.

$15.99

Sancocho

A hearty seven-meat stew with yuca, plantains, and corn. The ultimate comfort food, served with avocado and white rice.

$17.99
Breakfast

Los Tres Golpes

Mangú (mashed plantains) served with three hits: fried eggs, Dominican salami, and queso frito. The perfect Dominican breakfast.

$12.99

Tostones & Mojo

Twice-fried green plantain slices served with our signature garlic and olive oil mojo sauce. Crispy, savory perfection.
