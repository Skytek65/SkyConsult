Our Story

Bringing the Heart of India to Mason, Ohio

At A1 Indian Restaurant & Bar, we believe that great food is about more than just taste—it's about tradition, passion, and the joy of sharing a meal with loved ones. Our chefs bring decades of experience in authentic Indian cooking, using time-honored recipes and the freshest ingredients.

From our aromatic biryanis to our creamy butter chicken, every dish is prepared with care and attention to detail. We take pride in offering a warm, inviting atmosphere where families, friends, and food lovers can come together to enjoy the best Indian cuisine in Mason.
