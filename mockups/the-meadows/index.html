Our Story

A Legacy of Flavor & Family

Since 1934, The Meadows has been a beloved gathering place in Middletown, Ohio. Today, owner Ricky and his wife Chef Anna carry on this tradition, welcoming every guest like family into our cozy, historic dining room adorned with wooden paneling and photographs that tell our story.

Chef Anna thoughtfully prepares each dish with fresh ingredients and vibrant presentations. From our succulent Beef Udon Noodles to the exceptional Buddha Delight, every plate reflects her passion for blending Asian culinary traditions with American comfort food favorites.
