Our Story

Where Family,
Food & Heart Meet

Welcome to The Meadows, a cozy haven nestled in Middletown where Asian-inspired comfort food meets family warmth. Owner Anna personally prepares each dish in our kitchen, ensuring every plate carries the authentic flavors and vibrant presentations that have made us a community favorite.

Beyond the food, host Ricky adds a personal touch that makes every meal feel like a gathering with friends. From our curated wine selection to handcrafted cocktails, we've created an inviting atmosphere where memories are made and exceptional dining experiences await.
