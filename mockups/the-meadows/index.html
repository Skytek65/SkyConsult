Our Story

A Labor of Love, From Our Family to Yours

Nestled in the heart of Middletown, The Meadows is more than just a restaurant—it's a gathering place where memories are made over exceptional food. Owner and Chef Anna brings decades of culinary expertise to every dish, carefully preparing each meal to order with the freshest ingredients and time-honored techniques.

Her husband Ricky adds the warm, personal touch that makes every visit special. From the moment you walk through our doors, you're not just a guest—you're family. We take pride in knowing many of our regulars by name and remembering their favorite dishes.

Our limited hours aren't a limitation—they're a testament to our commitment. Anna arrives early each morning to craft her famous desserts from scratch, and every dish is prepared fresh when you order. Good food takes time, and we're worth the wait.
