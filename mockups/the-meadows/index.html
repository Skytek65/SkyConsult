Our Story

Family, Food &
Unforgettable Moments

Nestled in the heart of Middletown, OH, The Meadows is more than just a restaurant—it's a gathering place where friends become family and every meal tells a story.

Led by Chef Anna, each dish is thoughtfully prepared from scratch using fresh, quality ingredients. Her husband Ricky ensures every guest feels like part of the family, offering warm hospitality that keeps diners coming back.

Our Asian-American fusion celebrates the best of both worlds: traditional flavors with modern comfort, creating a dining experience that's as memorable as it is delicious.
