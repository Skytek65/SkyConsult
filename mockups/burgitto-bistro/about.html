Our Story

From a food truck dream in 2015 to a beloved brick-and-mortar destination in Morrow, Ohio. Family-owned, community-loved, and serving up the original Burgitto.

Humble Beginnings on Wheels

Burgitto Bistro started out in 2015 on a food truck where we began building a following that still raves about us to this day. We traveled throughout our hometown of Blanchester, Ohio, bringing our unique creations to the Clinton County Fair, Jeff Wyler's events, Blanchester's Fourth of July celebrations, Tractor Pulls, and the Clinton County Corn Festival.

On March 2nd, 2018, we opened our brick and mortar location in the Roachester area of Morrow, Ohio, bringing our food truck favorites to a permanent home.

Even with our new location, you may still find us at festivals when we can steal away from the bistro. We love staying connected to our roots and the community that helped us grow.

What Exactly is a Burgitto?

Our signature creation that started it all. It's not quite a burger, not quite a burrito—it's something entirely unique and delicious.

The Original

The Burgitto consists of a ¼ lb. steakburger, filled with classic vegetable fillings and your choice of American, Swiss, or pepper jack cheeses and condiments, all wrapped in a flour tortilla and grilled to perfection.

Cincinnati Style

We honor our Ohio roots with Cincinnati-style chili options, plus unique toppings like fried green tomatoes and shrimp for those looking to try something new.

Made Fresh

Every Burgitto is made to order and grilled until perfectly crispy on the outside while keeping all those delicious fillings warm and melty inside.
