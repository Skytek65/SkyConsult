Our Story

Burgitto Bistro started in 2015 on a food truck, building a following that raves high reviews wherever we go. From the Clinton County Fair to Jeff Wyler's and the Corn Festival, we've brought our unique flavors across Ohio.

On March 2nd, 2018, we opened our brick-and-mortar location in the Roachester area of Morrow, Ohio. Owned by Christina Price and managed by Shawn Felts, we're a family business serving up passion on every plate.

What Exactly is a Burgitto?

The burgitto consists of a ¼ lb steakburger, filled with a variety of toppings such as classic vegetable fillings, Cincinnati-style chili, shrimp, or even fried green tomatoes, plus American, Swiss or pepper jack cheeses and condiments. Wrapped in a flour tortilla and grilled to perfection.

The Classic

Our signature bacon cheese burgitto served with crispy waffle cut fries. The perfect introduction to our unique style.
