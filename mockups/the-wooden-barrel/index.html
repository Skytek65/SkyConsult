About Us

A Small Local Mom & Pop Place

At THE WOODEN BARREL, we believe in serving real fresh food made with love. We're not a chain restaurant - we're a family-owned local spot where you can enjoy hearty American comfort food that tastes like home.

From our hand-cut boneless chops to our hand-pattied burgers (yes, we have Goetta!), every dish is prepared fresh when you order it. Whether you're stopping by for breakfast, lunch, or dinner, you'll always find friendly service and generous portions.
