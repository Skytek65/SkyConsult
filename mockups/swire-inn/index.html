Our Story

Historic Charm,
Modern Taste

Nestled in a beautifully restored historic building in downtown Middletown, The Swire Inn brings the warmth and character of an authentic English pub to the heart of Ohio. Our reclaimed wooden bar, exposed brick, and cozy atmosphere create the perfect setting for memorable dining experiences.

Since opening our doors, we've become a hub for culinary creativity, serving elevated comfort food with a twist. From our inventive deviled egg variations to classic English favorites like Shepherd's Pie and Fish & Chips, every dish is crafted with care and served with hospitality that makes you feel at home.
