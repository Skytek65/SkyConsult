Our Story

A Historic Gathering Place

Nestled in a charming historic building on South Main Street, The Swire Inn has become Middletown's favorite destination for English-pub style comfort food. Our cozy atmosphere welcomes you with warm lighting, exposed brick, and the aroma of house-made specialties.

Whether you're joining us for our award-winning Fish & Chips, savoring our famous Deviled Eggs, or exploring our rotating seasonal cocktails, you'll discover why locals call us "a fabulous place to eat high-end food for a decent price."
