Our Story

A Historic Gathering Place

Nestled in the heart of downtown Middletown, The Swire Inn occupies a beautifully preserved historic building that has been transformed into a cozy, inviting gastropub. Our name pays homage to the building's rich heritage while our menu celebrates the best of English pub cuisine with an American twist.

Whether you're joining us for our famous Fish & Chips, award-winning Pot Roast, or handcrafted cocktails, you'll experience warm hospitality in a setting that blends old-world charm with modern comfort. Our rotating seasonal menu and daily chef specials ensure there's always something new to discover.
